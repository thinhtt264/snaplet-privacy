Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 19, 2025  ·  Last updated: May 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Snaplet ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application. By using Snaplet, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and username used to create your account
  • Profile content — profile photo and display name you choose to set
  • Media — photos, videos, and files you choose to upload or share within the app
  • Messages — chat messages sent and received through the app
  • Device token — Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token used to deliver push notifications
  • Device identifiers — anonymized device fingerprint for session security purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate and improve Snaplet:

  • Authenticate your identity and manage your session securely
  • Enable messaging and media sharing features between users
  • Deliver push notifications about activity relevant to you
  • Maintain app security and prevent unauthorized access

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Your data may be processed by trusted infrastructure providers (cloud storage, database hosting) solely for the purpose of operating the app. These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, your personal data and content will be permanently removed within 30 days.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), token-based authentication, and access controls to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by discontinuing use of the app

7. Children's Privacy

Snaplet is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app. Continued use of Snaplet after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
