Delete Account

Request account and data deletion

You can request the deletion of your Snaplet account and all associated data at any time. Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered.

How to Request Deletion

1
Send an email to us

Email thinhtruongtien264@gmail.com with the subject line: "Delete Account Request"

2
Include your account information

Provide the email address associated with your Snaplet account so we can identify and verify your request.

3
We process your request

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of receiving your request.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account profile (name, email, username, avatar)
  • All messages and chat history
  • All media you have uploaded (photos, videos, files)
  • Your posts and activity
  • Device tokens and session data

What May Be Retained

The following data may be retained for a limited period after deletion for legal or operational reasons:

  • Server logs (retained up to 90 days for security purposes)
  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics data that cannot identify you
