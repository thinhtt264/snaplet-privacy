Safety Standards

Child Safety & CSAM Prevention Policy · Effective: May 19, 2025

Snaplet is committed to providing a safe environment for all users and maintaining zero tolerance for any content that exploits or harms children.

1. Prohibited Content

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) of any kind is strictly prohibited
  • Any content that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers minors
  • Grooming behavior or any attempts to solicit minors
  • Content that promotes or normalizes harm to children

2. Enforcement Measures

  • All reported content is reviewed promptly and removed if it violates this policy
  • Accounts found violating this policy are permanently banned
  • We report confirmed CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant authorities
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations

3. Reporting Mechanism

Users can report any content or behavior that endangers children directly through the app or by contacting us:

4. Age Restriction

Snaplet is intended for users aged 13 and above. Users under 13 are not permitted to create accounts. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

5. Compliance

Snaplet complies with all applicable laws regarding child safety, including reporting obligations to relevant national and international authorities when CSAM is detected.
